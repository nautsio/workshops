Training

As one of the first Docker partners we are able to offer the official Docker training.

This training introduces you to the Docker platform and takes you through installing, integrating, and running it in your working environment.

Programme and Course Overview

You will benefit from this course if…

  • you are a administrator or developer,
  • you want to understand the use of Docker.

What you will achieve

After this training we will provide you with an official certificate of completion from Docker Inc.

After completing the 2-day training:

you will know:

  • the history of Docker,
  • the ‘why’ of Docker,
  • how to install the Docker Engine,
  • how to create your first Docker container,
  • how connect containers together (links),
  • container orchestration,
  • Docker Container Security,
  • Docker in Production (ambassadors, service discovery, etc.).
